BooleanMinds thrives to bridge the talent gap between educational institutes and corporates. We are passionate about training and put our heart and soul in injecting technical skills in an individual. With the same zeal and passion, BooleanMinds offer Campus to Corporate services to corporates.

What's It

Hire Smart Freshers from Campus

We have a stringent selection process that includes aptitude and technical evaluation with combination of written and verbal tests, to select the smartest from the crowd.

Groom the Freshers for 3-6 Months

Customized training plan to suite the needs of the corporates. The trianing plan covers not only technical topics, but also soft skills and communication skills required.

Deploy On Projects for Corporates

Our systematic and strict evaluation process ensures that the candidate is ready to be deployed on the project right from day 1 of finishing the grooming period.

 
 

How We Do It

Companies provide us with job description of the Candidates as well as information on no. of candidates, colleges they would prefer and their key skills.

We run campus drive in colleges and select the best ones. We also take fresh graduates who are already out of the college for less than a year.

We provide rigorous training to the candidates for 3-6 months. Along with classroom lecture, sufficient hands-on project work and assignments is given to the candidates.
